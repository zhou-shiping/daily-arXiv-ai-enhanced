To address this issue, we develop a phase-space parametrization that accurately generates the distributions of forward-emitted charged leptons. We modify the \HELAS\ library to evaluate helicity amplitudes in the singular region so that vertices at very small invariant momentum square of order $m_e^2$ can be accurately evaluated even at multi-TeV energies.
